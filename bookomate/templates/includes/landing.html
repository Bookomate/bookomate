How It Started

The project was first came about by Kalle Hallden. He tried to start a Open-Source Project to build a meeting app. This project never took off. Then we started Bookomate after Kalle Bookomatic. Ever since we kept build on the idea of an open source meeting app.

Our End Goal

Are end goal is to create a fully functioning meeting app that is open source and free to all.

Something Else

Everyone is allowed to help build up this website to be the best. We do not track you, or mine your data. We believe in privacy and security
